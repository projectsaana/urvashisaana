About Urvashi

THE VOICE
BEHIND
THE SOUND

Urvashi Saana is an Indian singer-songwriter and digital music creator born from the valleys of Jammu & Kashmir — where every lyric carries the weight of mountains and the hush of snow-quiet mornings.

Her music lives at the edge of indie-pop, electronic, and cinematic — shifting between soulful ballads and upbeat electronic tracks without ever losing her identity. Since 2021, she has collaborated across borders and earned a consistent 5-star record that speaks louder than any bio.

She thinks and feels what no one else can. That is her voice. That is her art.
